Wild Ewes Woolery - 3rd Party Logistics is at the forefront of modern logistics, combining advanced technology with a commitment to customer satisfaction. Our mission is to provide reliable, efficient, and cost-effective logistics solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Based in California, we have built a reputation for excellence in the industry.

Staffed with an experienced and talented group of dedicated employees, Wild Ewes Woolery - 3rd Party Logistics is committed to providing superior, innovative and cost-effective Transportation and Logistics solutions for its clients. We are customer focused and we strive to produce solutions that improve our customers supply chain efficiency. Our staff has over 215 Years of combined Industry experience!

Join the growing number of businesses that trust Wild Ewes Woolery - 3rd Party Logistics for their logistics needs. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help streamline your supply chain and enhance your business operations.

Our hours of operation are Monday through Friday, 7 AM Pacific Standard Time to 5 PM Pacific Standard Time. Our “On Call” attendant is available during all “Off Hours” including holidays and weekends. Please note that our “On Call” service is EXCLUSIVELY available ONLY to existing and current customers. We are available for personalized customer service 24 hours a day, 365 days a year!

Today, the company continues to be a family owned enterprise and operates as Wild Ewes Woolery - 3rd Party Logistics offering custom logistics services, including domestic LTL, FTL, Partial Loads, Container Drayage, Curbside Delivery, White Glove Service, Exclusive Use Vehicles, Inbound and Reverse Logistics, and complete International Air, Ocean and Customs House Brokerage.
